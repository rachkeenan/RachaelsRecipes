Chili Con Carne

Chili Con Carne is the perfect comfort dish, it is packed with vegetables and flavors, and is very easy to make.

⏱️ Prep Time: 40mins | ⏱️ Cooking Time: 60mins

Ingredients

1/4 pint/150ml of water
1 dessertspoon of flour
1 clove garlic
1 teaspoon chilli powder
1 red pepper, sliced
1lb/450g lean minced lamb/beef
1 onion, finely chopped
1 medium carrot, sliced
2 dessertspoons peas
5 mushrooms, sliced
1/2 tin tomatoes
2 dessertspoons tomato sauce
1/2 tin kidney beans
1 low salt beef stock cube

Method

  1. Chop onion and garlic. Dissolve beef stock cube in 150ml of boiling water.
  2. Put minced beef/lamb in a dry frying pan. Cook until well browned, stirring all the time. Drain off excess from the pan.
  3. Add onions and garlic to the pan and cook for 2-3 mins. Sprinkle on the flour and chilli powder and cook for 1-2 mins, stirring all the time.
  4. Add beef stock, tomato sauce and tinned tomatoes. Bring to the boil.
  5. Add kidney beans, carrot, peas, mushrooms and pepper to the mince. Simmer for 20mins.

Equipment Needed:

  • Deep frying pan, with lid or tin foil to cover
