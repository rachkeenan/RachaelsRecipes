Double Chocolate Chip Cookies

These delicious double chocolate chip cookies have a soft center and crunchy outer.

⏱️ Prep Time: 10mins | ⏱️ Baking Time: 10mins

Ingredients

115g softened butter
100g light brown sugar
75g granulated sugar
1tsp vanilla extract
1 egg
150g all purpose flour (plain flour)
30g cocoa powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
85g white chocolate chips
175g milk chocolate chips
100g smarties (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 175ºC/350ºF
  2. Put the softened butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar into the bowl of your stand mixer. Beat them together until the mixture is lighter and everything is well combined. (about 3 minutes)
  3. Add the vanilla extract and the egg, then beat once more until everything is creamy and even lighter in colour. (This takes about 3-4 minutes)
  4. Add in the flour, cocoa powder, salt and baking soda, then mix until the flour is just combined.
  5. Tip in the the white and milk chocolate chips into the mixture.
  6. Then mix until everything is combined.
  7. Roll golf ball sized balls of dough and place them onto a baking sheet lined with cooking paper. Ensure they are 5cm (2 inches) apart
  8. Bake for 10 minutes until lightly golden. (They will be soft when you take them from the oven!)
  9. Carefully transfer the cookies still on the cooking paper to a cooling rack, then use a spoon to gently flatten the still soft cookies.
  10. Leave the cookies to cool.

Equipment Needed

  • Large mixing bowl (if not using a stand mixer)
  • Wooden spoon or spatula, for mixing
  • Baking trays
  • Grease proof paper
  • Cooling rack
